Our Services

The tool provides users with real-time feedback on their submitted reviews, analyzing the text, audio, and video to classify the sentiment as positive, negative, or neutral. Though general-purpose, the application can be fine-tuned or customized for specific domains, such as customer service, social media, e-commerce reviews, or corporate feedback. The tool can summarize the overall sentiment from multiple reviews into a comprehensive report, helping businesses gauge general user sentiment for specific products or services over time. In addition to classifying sentiment as positive, negative, or neutral, the tool can also provide a scale of emotional intensity, indicating how strongly the user feels about the subject. The service can preprocess user input by cleaning, correcting, or tokenizing the text, allowing for more accurate sentiment predictions.
